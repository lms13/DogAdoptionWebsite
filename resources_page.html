Common Necessities

Remember to buy these necessary items before you new pup gets home:

  • Collar
  • Food & Water bowls
  • A bed
  • Tooth-cleaning Bone
  • Puppy Pee Pads
  • Healthcare & Cleaning supplies
  • Treats to reward your new pup!
  • Remember your new dog needs lots of time and attention!
  • Ensure you do some pre adoption research on the items listed above to validate they are available in your area!

Pet Care

Make sure your new adoption is most comfortable!

  • Do you have any other pets at home?
  • If so, will your new adoption be compatible with other pets at home?
  • Ensure that there is no one with allergies to your new dog at home.
  • Take note of the breed of dog you are adopting and ensure your living arrangements are appropriate.
  • Ensure you have a plan for the dogs care in case you are absent for several days.
  • Make sure to be patient with your new adoption, especially if it is a puppy!
  • Remember, pet care includes quality time and emotional support!
